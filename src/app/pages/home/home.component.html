Welcome to Moodify

An app that uses machine learning algorithms to guess your mood based on your recently played songs and creates a playlist of songs that match your mood.

How It Works

1. Log In

Log in with your Spotify account to access your recently played songs.

2. Analyze Your Mood

Using machine learning algorithms, the app analyzes your recently played songs and guesses your mood.

3. Create Your Playlist

The app creates a playlist of songs that match your mood, which you can then save to your Spotify account.
